Our Heritage

A Quarter Century of Restraint

Founded in a small atelier in Jakarta, Maison Lumière was built on a single conviction: that true luxury whispers. We have never advertised, and we never will. Our clients find us through those who already belong.

Every creation passes through the hands of a single artisan, from the first sketch to the final polish — a continuity of care that machines cannot replicate.
